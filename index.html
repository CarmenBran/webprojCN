Our Story

Hello and welcome to A's Corner. I am Carmen, I am originally from Romania, but I now live in the UK with my partner, Bryan, and our two little girls. (Alessia and Amy, get it?) Becoming a mother was the one thing I didn't know I needed until I had it, I love is so much, but it's so draining. They require all of my attention 24/7, and that makes me a bit tense. One evening I was scrolling through Youtube and I found a video with somebody doing polymer clay earrings, and I said, what in the world is that? I think, that was the moment Bryan realised our pockets will get slightly lighter and cabinets slightly fuller, and boy was he right. I started researching more and more about this wonderful thing I used to play with when I was a kid and absolutely loved it, and shortly after, I had everything I needed to start. And so the journey had begun.

Whilst Amy is too small to give an opinion (not born yet), Alessia loves to pick out colours, to play with my tools which is always fun to supervise, she particularly enjoys mixing the clay.
Having my daughter interested in what I do and trying to help me or just mess about brings me so much joy, because I know, in the long term, we will be bonding together over this, it will be our little thing to share, no boys involved haha.

Soon after the bundle arrived, (few hours later), the first batch with one singular earring was ready, the result: horrific, and I couldn't be prouder haha. I'm getting sidetracked, the point was polymer clay became therapeutic to some degree and my "me" time, it takes a lot of time, practice, materials, but you'll love the products, and you have cute Christmas gifts too.

Thank you for taking the time to read about us and who we are, we love connecting with people all over the world and we are hoping to receive a message from you, with questions or just to say hi.

Have a wonderful day!
